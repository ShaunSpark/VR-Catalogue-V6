Know what you want?

Request a Specific App

Please review the requirements below before submitting. The Spark team aims to respond within 5 working days.

🥽
Meta Quest Compatible
The app must be available on Meta Quest 2, 3 or Pro — or have a confirmed roadmap for Meta Quest support. PC-only or tethered-only apps are not currently supported.
🎓
Academic Relevance
The app should align with at least one DCU module, programme or transversal skill area. Please indicate the subject area and the specific learning outcomes it supports.
👥
Group or Individual Use
The app should be suitable for either a supervised group session (10–30 students) or structured individual use. Apps requiring persistent accounts or external data are subject to additional review.
⚖️
Appropriate Content
Content must be appropriate for a university audience. Apps containing graphic violence, gambling mechanics, or content unrelated to education will not be approved.
💶
Cost Transparency
Please provide the pricing model — free, one-off purchase, or subscription. Institutional pricing is preferred. High-cost enterprise apps may be flagged for pilot review before full approval.
🔒
Data & Privacy
The app must not require students to create personal accounts unless a shared institutional login is available. Data collection should be minimal and GDPR-compliant.
